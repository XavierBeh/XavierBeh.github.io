OUR story

Ambition Born Out Of Hardship

Fragrance Hair Saloon launched at 2013, founded by Ray Ho and his wife, Yumi. where both of them run the business together. For the sake of improving his parents’ life, Ray Ho decided to acquire hair cut skills and launched his own salon, entering the field of entrepreneurship.

The name Fragrance originated from the most popular colour in hairdressing trends in 2013-purple. At that time, in many Beauty Fields competitions, purple works accounted for two-thirds. Therefore, the founder and his wife decided to use Fragrance as the name of the store, and the decoration of the store was also set with a lavender theme. This is because lavender is also predominantly purple. So far, customers also call us "Purple Hair Salon". But now we are in the process of transitioning our branding where we decided to change our brand colours to gold.
